Creative website publishing

Koken is a free system designed for photographers, designers, and creative DIYs to publish independent websites of their work.

Live chat happening now  Questions? Click here to speak with the developers.

Library

Exceptional media management

Koken manages your uploaded images and videos through a dedicated, desktop-like interface.

Unlisted content

Shareable content links accessible only to clients and contacts. May unlist albums, sets, as well as individual media.

Collections

Group media with albums, then group albums with sets. Media is independent and may be shared across multiple albums.

Favorites, featured

Mark media as featured or favorite to group together the cream-of-the-crop for your published website.

User ordering

Drag and drop media inside albums into your preferred order. Public albums and sets may be manually ordered just the same.

Tags & categories

Everything can be tagged and categorized. Images with IPTC are automatically classified using their metadata after upload.

Replaceable content

Upload new versions of existing images and videos anytime. All media data and site links are maintained.

Text

Work and words, together

Write about portfolio updates, inspiration, or anything that comes to mind. Images, videos, slideshows and even content from Flickr, Instagram, Vimeo, SoundCloud and Twitter are a snap to display.

Site

Live site previewing and editing

Customize your website's style inside the console using simple style controls or write your own CSS.

Settings panel

Change a theme's color, layout, typography and more with a point-and-click panel of options.

Custom CSS editor

Append a custom style sheet to any theme. Make edits alongside the site preview and see your changes live.

Drafts

Site edits are saved as drafts automatically and may be reverted to anytime without affecting your live site.

Navigation builder

Link to internal or external content with navigation links. Easy to add, remove and sort via drag and drop.

Live preview

See all your site edits instantly inside the console. May also compare edits with your live site.

Filters

Edit page content order and visibility using tags, categories, favorites and featured criteria.
